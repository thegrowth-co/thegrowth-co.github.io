Our story

Built to fix fragmented marketing.

TheGrowthCo. was founded by Mohammed Sohail Sayyed to solve a problem he saw repeatedly among small and mid-sized businesses across Navi Mumbai — fragmented marketing. A website built by one freelancer, SEO handled by another, social media managed by a third, with no one connecting the strategy across channels.

TheGrowthCo. was built around a simple idea: businesses grow faster when their website, SEO, advertising and content all move in the same direction, guided by one accountable partner. Since our founding, we've delivered 20+ projects across 4+ industries, developed 7+ business websites, and maintained a 98% client satisfaction rate.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Quick answers to what businesses ask most before starting a project with us.

Do you only work with businesses in Ulwe and Navi Mumbai?

No. While we're based in Ulwe and have strong local market knowledge, we work with businesses across India remotely, using video calls, shared documents and regular reporting to keep every project on track.

What makes TheGrowthCo. different from a typical freelancer or large agency?

Unlike a freelancer, we offer a structured process, a full range of integrated services and reliable turnaround times. Unlike a large agency, you get direct access to the people actually working on your project — no layers of account managers or inflated retainers.

Do you offer a free consultation before starting a project?

Yes. Every relationship starts with a free consultation where we understand your business, answer your questions and outline a suggested approach — with no obligation to proceed.

How involved will I need to be during the project?

We aim to keep the process efficient for busy business owners. Your input matters during discovery and key approvals, but day-to-day execution is handled by our team, with regular updates.

What industries do you have the most experience in?

We've worked across real estate, healthcare, education, retail, hospitality, home services, professional services and startups — giving us a broad understanding of how different marketing strategies apply across sectors.
